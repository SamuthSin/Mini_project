About Us

Welcome to Coffee Time!

Nestled in the heart of Cambodia, Coffee Time is more than just a place to grab a coffee—it's a community hub where people come to relax, connect, and enjoy high-quality brews. Since we opened our doors in 2002, our mission has been simple: to create an inviting space where everyone can experience the art of coffee and a little warmth with every cup.

Our Coffee Philosophy

We believe that great coffee starts with the beans. That's why we source only the finest, ethically-grown beans from around the world, ensuring that each cup tells a story of dedication and craftsmanship. Our skilled baristas are passionate about every pour, carefully crafting each drink to bring out the best flavors and aromas.

Our Promise to You

At Coffee Time, we're dedicated to:

  • Using only the best beans and ingredients, ensuring each drink meets our high standards.
  • Committing to eco-friendly practices, from our sourcing to our waste reduction efforts.
  • Supporting local artists, creators, and organizations to keep our community vibrant and connected.

Our Menu

We offer a wide range of beverages and treats, from classic espresso drinks to unique seasonal creations. And don’t forget to pair your coffee with one of our freshly-baked pastries, made daily with love.
